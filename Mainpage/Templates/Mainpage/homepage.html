Global Location

Ryonan Denso Company Ltd. Japan

Founded in Japan, Ryonan Denso Company Ltd. serves as the headquarters of Ryonan Electric, where the company’s journey began. This facility oversees research, development, and strategic direction for all global locations. Known for its commitment to innovation and quality, the Himeji office has been at the forefront of product development, providing state-of-the-art wire harness solutions and other electronic components to a wide range of industries.

Ryonan Electric Vietnam Co.,LTD.

Opened as part of Ryonan Electric’s expansion into Southeast Asia, the Vietnam facility was established to meet growing demand in the region. Located in Hanoi’s Thang Long Industrial Park, this facility focuses on manufacturing and assembly, ensuring efficient delivery of high-quality products to regional clients. The Vietnam branch reflects Ryonan's commitment to regional markets and provides valuable job opportunities, supporting the local economy and enhancing Ryonan’s capacity to meet diverse client needs.

Ryonan Electric India Private Limited

The India branch is Ryonan Electric's primary location for South Asia operations, established in Gurugram’s IMT Manesar to cater to the automotive and electronics industries in the region. This facility is a hub for innovation and is equipped with advanced manufacturing technology to deliver products that meet international quality standards. Ryonan Electric India plays a critical role in expanding the company’s market presence in South Asia, ensuring close collaboration with key industry players in India.

Izumo Factory

Located in Shimane Prefecture, the Izumo Factory is a core manufacturing site that combines advanced production capabilities with strict quality control. Since its establishment, the Izumo facility has been essential in supporting Ryonan Electric’s global supply chain, producing high-performance components with precision and care. This location continues to uphold Ryonan’s standards of excellence and helps to drive forward the company's reputation for quality and reliability in wire harness and electronic components.
